Our Fleet

Next-generation launch vehicles engineered for performance

SkyLynx-1

Light-Lift Vehicle — The first rocket of our company. Capable of getting ~25 tons of payload to orbit. Has completed 9 flights including a successful Mars delivery with the Socrates mission.

SkyLynx-9

Medium-Lift Reusable Vehicle — Our Falcon 9-inspired two-stage rocket featuring a reusable first stage booster with autonomous landing capability. Designed for high-cadence satellite deployment and crew transport missions.

Super Heavy-Lift Fully Reusable Vehicle — Our most ambitious vehicle yet, inspired by Starship. Both the booster and upper stage are designed for full and rapid reusability. Two flight tests completed with booster and upper stage recovery under active development.
